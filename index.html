About Belgium

A small but powerful country in Western Europe.
Known for waffles, fries, chocolate, medieval towns,
and as the headquarters of the European Union 🇪🇺.

History

Belgium became independent in 1830.
It is a constitutional monarchy with a king 👑.
Today, King Philippe is the monarch.

Culture

Belgium is famous for art (Magritte 🎨),
comics (Tintin 📚), festivals and music 🎶.

Food

Belgian food is delicious:
🧇 Waffles, 🍟 Fries, 🍫 Chocolate, 🍺 Beer.
